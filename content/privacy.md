**Effective date:** May 2026

Gentry Remodeling LLC, DBA Diagnostic Plumbing ("we," "us," or "our") operates dplumbaz.com. This page explains what information we collect when you use our website, how we use it, and your choices.

---

## Information We Collect

When you submit a request-for-service or contact form on this site, we collect:

- **Name** (required)
- **Phone number** (required)
- **Email address** (optional)
- **Service description or message** (optional)
- **New or returning client status** (optional, homepage form only)

We do not use advertising cookies or third-party analytics other than the Meta Pixel (Facebook/Meta), which is used for conversion tracking and ad optimization. The Meta Pixel is deployed site-wide via our website's `<head>` tag. We do NOT collect payment information through this site.

---

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information you submit to:

- Contact you to schedule an estimate or service appointment
- Follow up on a quote or service request
- Maintain a record of your service history

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with marketers or unrelated third parties.

---

## Third-Party Processors

Form submissions are processed by **JobTread**, our customer relationship management (CRM) platform. When you submit a form, your information is transmitted directly to JobTread's servers at `app.jobtread.com`. JobTread's privacy practices are governed by their own privacy policy, available at [jobtread.com](https://www.jobtread.com).

We do not operate a separate database on this website. All submitted information resides in JobTread.

---

## Data Retention

Your information is retained in JobTread for as long as needed to manage your service relationship with us. If you would like your information removed, contact us using the information below and we will delete it promptly.
