### What Makes Us Different

**Water Quality:** We review each citys annually published water lab test data. We strongly believe this is the only way to get a full picture of the contaminates we need to optimize for when speccing water filtration systems. An in-home water test has its place, but cities source from different water sources throughout the year. A single test on a single day will not demonstrate the full range of contaminants.

**Drain Cleaning:** We offer traditional and high-speed mechanical drain cleaning: a cable-driven system that restores the pipe to its original diameter instead of just punching a hole through buildup.
