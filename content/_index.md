Diagnostic Plumbing is a family-owned and operated plumbing and general contracting company based in Queen Creek, serving the East Valley since Gary Witkowski started the business. (CLAUDE)

We work in Gilbert, Chandler, Mesa, Scottsdale, Tempe, Queen Creek, San Tan Valley, and Phoenix. Most of our calls come from neighbors who found us through someone they trust, and we work hard to keep it that way.

### What Makes Us Different

East Valley water is hard — Gilbert averages 13 grains per gallon, and some Mesa neighborhoods peak at 25. We got serious about water quality early, and we use actual lab data to match the right treatment to what's in your specific water, not a one-size-fits-all sales pitch.

On drain cleaning, we were among the first in the East Valley to offer high-speed mechanical drain cleaning — a cable-driven system that restores the pipe to its original diameter instead of just punching a hole through buildup. It costs more than a standard snake, but clogs come back less often.

We're also triple-licensed: plumbing (ROC #327364), carpentry and remodeling (ROC #327365), and general remodeling (ROC #332463). That last one — a B-3 license — lets us general-contract full bathroom and kitchen remodels without bringing in a separate GC. One company, one point of contact, one set of standards.
