## Areas We Serve Across the Phoenix East Valley

Diagnostic Plumbing is a locally owned company serving homeowners and businesses throughout Maricopa and Pinal Counties. From the established neighborhoods of Mesa and Tempe to the fast-growing communities of Gilbert, Chandler, and Queen Creek, we bring the same triple-licensed crew to every job — CR-37 Plumbing (#327364), B-3 General Remodeling (#332463), and CR-61 Carpentry (#327365).

The East Valley shares a few things in common no matter which city you call home: notoriously hard water that tests above 200 mg/L, homes built during the 2000s boom now reaching the age where water heaters and supply lines need attention, and a climate that's hard on plumbing systems year-round. We know these conditions because we live and work here.
