East Valley tap water is among the hardest in the country, and hardness is just one of the issues. **Gilbert's arsenic reaches the federal legal limit. Tempe's PFOS is 750% of the 2029 federal MCL. San Tan Valley's nitrate sits at 93% of the maximum contaminant level.** Hardness itself runs from 13 grains per gallon (gpg) in Gilbert to peaks of 33 in Tempe groundwater blends.

No single technology addresses all of that. The right setup for most East Valley homes is layered: a softener for hardness throughout the house, a carbon stage for chlorine and disinfection byproducts, and an under-sink reverse osmosis system for dissolved contaminants like arsenic, PFAS, nitrate, TDS that nothing else touches.
