**Question: *What about whats coming out of my tap?*** Contamination (such as agricultural runoff) and treatment (such as adding chlorine) happens at the source, not in the pipes between the source and your tap. We can absolutely perform an in-home test at your tap, and for some homes that's the right decision. But an in-home test will yield results within the city's provided range, leading to the same configuration recommendation. **In fact, a clients needs and goals influence the design of the system more than for example, wether the arsenic level is 5 or 8 ppb -- we'll be targeting arsenic with the same effective method in either case.**
